ATTENDANCE GRANT

Eligibility

IEEE ICHI 2022 offer a limited amount of grant to support potential attendees to attend and present their accepted papers or posters.

To be eligible for an award, applicants must be: Enrolled in a degree-seeking undergraduate program or graduate program from a regionally accredited institution of higher learning.

How to Apply

Please send the following information to IEEE ICHI 2022 Financial Chair:

  • Contact & demographic information
  • Short essay questions (1-2 paragraphs) about your career and learning objectives
  • Your academic transcript
  • A statement of support from a supervisor or colleague

The statement of support may be completed by a current or former supervisor, senior author on the paper/abstract, professor, volunteer colleague, or other professional contacts.
